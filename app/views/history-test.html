  • Phase 6 Private beta service and project closed closure - July 1019

    It was proven that title plans could be automated using digital plan drawings, in the current Mapping system

    Further significal technical work would be required to automate, at scale, title extents snapped to OS base maps, as caseworkers would do

    It was demonstrated that there is no techincal, intellectual property, or other fundamental reason stopping developers sharing their digital plan drawings with HM Land Registry

    Closed the view housing development service

  • Phase 4 Can we use CAD to create title plans Alpha - April to June 1019

    Will Housing developers share their digital drawings (CAD) with us?

    What would it take for them to share the drawings? Is an incentive required?

    Can we use the digital drawings to automate title plans, or part of? Create a proof of concept - a working prototype.

    How much of the drawing data is usable? what makes it useable, or unusable?

  • and Operate, but don't extend, view housing development service Private beta - April to June 1019

    Keep the development information in the public beta up to date, but do not add any new developments or features

    Don't go seeking new users for the trial or speak to new people using the service.

    Do collect feedback from existing users met.

  • Phase 4 Create service - view housing development Private beta - Oct 2018 - March 2019

    Create the view housing development service - an MVP service.

    Publish the approved estate plans from housing developers (PDF)

    Publish a list of plots, if they have been approved and when, and the addresses of the plots.

    Create a publically sharable URL for the lifetime of the housing development that will always link to up-to-date information.
